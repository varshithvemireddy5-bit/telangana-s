Fish of Telangana

The Murrel

1. State Fish of Telangana: Korra Meenu (Striped Murrel) Scientific Name: Channa striatus. Local Names: It is popularly known as Korra Meenu or Korra Matta in Telugu. Cultural & Medicinal Significance: Fish Prasadam: This fish is famous globally because of the Bathini Goud family in Hyderabad, who use live fingerlings of this species to administer a traditional herbal "medicine" for asthma patients. Culinary Staple: It is highly valued for its firm texture, fewer bones, and unique taste, making it a premium delicacy in Telangana cuisine. Resilience: It is an air-breathing fish, allowing it to survive in low-oxygen waters or even out of water for short periods. This resilience reflects the spirit of the state.
